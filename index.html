About Me

Who I am and what I do

I'm Ahmed Maher, a data analyst. I work on collecting, cleaning, and transforming data into interactive reports and dashboards that support decisions and help achieve business goals.

I have experience using advanced tools such as Python, SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau to analyze data statistically and present it in a simple and professional manner.

I have implemented various projects such as sales analysis, financial reporting, and customer dashboards, always focusing on the accuracy of results and ease of understanding.

I excel at handling raw data (data wrangling), creating analytical models, and using equations and statistical tools to illustrate patterns and trends.

I constantly strive to develop my skills through practical projects and training courses in the field of data science, and my passion is providing innovative solutions that help my clients make the most of their data.
